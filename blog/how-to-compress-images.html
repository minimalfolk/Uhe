Common Image Compression Questions

What's the best image format for Instagram?

JPEG is ideal for photos. Use PNG only for graphics with transparency. Instagram converts all uploads to JPEG anyway.

How much can I compress an image before quality loss?

Typically 70-90% of original quality is safe. ReducePic's Smart Compression automatically stops before visible quality loss.

Why does Instagram compress my images?
