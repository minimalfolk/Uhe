How to Reduce Image Size?

  1. Upload an image by clicking or dragging it to the upload box.
  2. Adjust the compression level to your desired percentage.
  3. Select your preferred format (JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP).
  4. Click "Reduce Now" to start the compression.
  5. Download your optimized image instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What formats can I upload? JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP.
  • What is the max file size? Up to 50MB.
  • Will my images lose quality? No! Our tool ensures the best balance between size and quality.
