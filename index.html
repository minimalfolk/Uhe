How to Reduce Image Size

  1. Upload your image by clicking or dragging it to the upload box.
  2. Choose the compression method (percentage or custom size).
  3. Click "Reduce Now" to start the compression process.
  4. Once compressed, you can download or share the image.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What formats can I upload? We support JPG, PNG, and WebP.
  • What is the maximum file size? The maximum file size is 10MB.
  • Can I undo the compression? Once compressed, the changes cannot be undone, but you can always compress again with different settings.
